Our Mission

Our organization is dedicated to empowering kids and youths among Refugees and Host Communities in Kenya by providing them with opportunities to excel in sports like basketball and football, as well as nurture their artistic talents in drawing.
Our mission is to create a safe and inclusive environment where they can develop essential life skills, build self-confidence, and foster a sense of belonging. Through our programs, we aim to promote teamwork, discipline, and resilience, while instilling a love for sports and the arts.
We believe that every child deserves a chance to discover and unleash their full potential, and we are committed to making a positive impact in their lives.

Our Vision

Our vision is to inspire a generation of young individuals Refugees and Host Communities in Kenya who are not only physically active but also creatively expressive.
We envision a society where kids and youths, regardless of their background, have equal access to sports and artistic opportunities.
By providing them with quality coaching and mentorship, we aim to cultivate their talents, nurture their aspirations, and empower them to become leaders in their communities. We strive to create a lasting impact by fostering a love for sports and the arts, promoting healthy lifestyles, and building a supportive network that encourages personal growth and achievement.

Our Values

our core values guide everything we do:
Empowerment: We believe in empowering children and youths, giving them the tools and opportunities to reach their full potential.
Inclusivity: We foster an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome, valued, and respected, regardless of their background or abilities.
Community: We believe in the power of community and strive to build strong connections among participants, volunteers, and supporters, creating a network of support and belonging.
Resilience: We encourage resilience and determination, helping children and youths overcome obstacles, build confidence, and develop essential life skills.
Creativity: We embrace the power of creativity and self-expression through sports and arts, allowing individuals to explore their talents, ideas, and passions.
Impact: We are committed to making a positive impact in the lives of children and youths, their families, and the communities we serve, creating lasting change for a brighter future.

Our History

Kuzakids organization was formed in October 2022 with the goal of supporting kids and youths in both refugee and host communities through sports and arts activities. Despite being relatively new, we are driven by a deep passion to make a positive impact in the lives of these young individuals. We understand the unique challenges they face and are committed to providing them with a safe and nurturing environment where they can grow, express themselves, and build essential life skills.
Though we may be young as an organization, our dedication and enthusiasm drive us to create meaningful opportunities for these kids and youths.
